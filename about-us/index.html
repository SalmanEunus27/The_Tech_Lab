About Us

The Tech Lab endeavors to create a whole new generation of tech innovators and developers in Bangladesh and ultimately, in the world. Being the first of it’s kind in the country and in the world, it provides Engineering education such as Programming, Electronics and Robotics to the children and youths in the country.
